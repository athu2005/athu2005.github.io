EcoCombust - Clean Tech Startup

Pre-Incubated at DSSE IIT Bombay | Retrofit Kit for Diesel Engines

Startup Overview

EcoCombust is a sustainability-focused startup pre-incubated at the DSSE (Department of Social Science and Entrepreneurship) at IIT Bombay. Backed by Rs. 4.75 Lakh funding under the IDEAS Program, we're developing a revolutionary retrofit kit for diesel engines that achieves 50% higher efficiency and 30% lower emissions with seamless retrofitting capability.

The Problem

  • Diesel Engine Inefficiency: Most diesel engines operate at 35-40% thermal efficiency, wasting 60% energy as heat
  • Emissions Challenge: Diesel engines are major contributors to NOx and particulate matter pollution
  • Replacement Cost Barrier: Fleet owners cannot afford to replace functioning engines; they need retrofitting solutions
  • Market Size: Millions of diesel engines in India across commercial vehicles, generators, and industrial equipment

EcoCombust Solution

Our patent-pending mono-fuel six-stroke combustion cycle retrofit kit enables:

  • 50% Higher Efficiency: Optimized combustion and thermal recovery through novel six-stroke configuration
  • 30% Lower Emissions: Improved combustion control and thermal management reduce harmful emissions
  • Seamless Retrofitting: Bolt-on installation without major engine modification or downtime
  • Cost-Effective: Payback period through fuel savings achievable within 2-3 years

Technical Innovation

The core innovation involves:

  • Camshaft System: Custom-designed six-stroke cam profiles leveraging research from our engine design project
  • Thermal Recovery: Integrated heat recovery system for waste heat utilization
  • Control Electronics: Smart timing control adapting combustion to engine load and fuel composition
  • Emissions Management: Advanced aftertreatment integration for meeting emission standards

Development Status

Current Phase: Proof-of-Concept Development

  • Laboratory testing of core components completed
  • Field trials with industrial partner diesel generators planned Q3 2025
  • Real-world performance validation across different load conditions
  • Market validation through target customer interviews and surveys

Business Model

Target Market Segments:

  • Commercial fleet operators (trucks, buses, tempo)
  • Industrial equipment manufacturers (diesel generators, pumps)
  • Construction equipment rental companies
  • Agricultural machinery operators

Competitive Advantages

  • Patent Protection: Novel combustion cycle design with patent applications filed
  • Research Backing: Built on rigorous IIT Bombay research and validation
  • Energy Efficiency Focus: Addresses primary pain point of fuel costs for fleet operators
  • Regulatory Alignment: Helps customers meet stringent emission standards
  • Low-Cost Retrofit Design: Minimal installation complexity compared to complete engine replacement
