EcoCombust

Clean-Tech Startup | Pre-incubated with Rs. 4.75L IDEAS Funding

Overview

Founded and led EcoCombust, a pre-incubated clean-technology startup developing advanced retrofit combustion systems for legacy vehicle and industrial engine fleets. Secured Rs. 4.75 lakhs IDEAS grant funding. Technology achieves 50% fuel efficiency improvement and 30% emissions reduction through proprietary combustion chamber redesign and thermal recovery integration.

Technology Innovation

Combustion System Architecture

Engineered novel combustion chamber geometry with integrated pre-chamber ignition system and charge stratification. Design improves flame propagation velocity and turbulent kinetic energy distribution, enabling leaner combustion at higher efficiency without sacrificing torque output. System compatible with existing engine platforms through modular retrofit design requiring minimal mechanical modifications.

Emission Control Integration

Integrated advanced exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) pathway with embedded catalytic converter. Cooled EGR reduces NOx formation at source while maintaining combustion completeness. Post-combustion treatment catalysts target PM and unburned hydrocarbon elimination, achieving Euro VI compliance on existing four-stroke engines.

Thermal Energy Recovery

Developed waste heat recovery system capturing energy from exhaust stream through integrated heat exchanger. Recovered thermal energy enhances oil warming and reduces cold-start inefficiencies. System design minimizes backpressure penalties through optimized flow path geometry.

Performance Validation

  • Efficiency Gains: 50% fuel efficiency improvement demonstrated on baseline test vehicle
  • Emission Reduction: 30% reduction in CO2-equivalent emissions across EPA test cycle
  • Torque Retention: Maintained peak torque output while broadening power delivery curve
  • Reliability Testing: 10,000 km field validation without performance degradation
  • Retrofit Compatibility: Successfully integrated on 5 distinct engine platforms

Business Development

Funding & Support

Secured Rs. 4.75 lakh IDEAS (Innovation and Development through Entrepreneurship Advancement Scheme) grant from Department of Science & Technology, Government of India. Recipient of pre-incubation infrastructure support through IIT Bombay entrepreneurship ecosystem. Grant funding accelerated prototype development and field validation timeline.

Market Positioning

Identified addressable market of 200+ million legacy vehicles requiring emission retrofitting across South Asia. Targeting commercial fleet operators and industrial equipment manufacturers as anchor customers. Regulatory compliance strategies adapted for regional emission standards (BS VI, Euro VI equivalent).

Go-to-Market Strategy

Established partnerships with certified service centers for retrofit installation and warranty management. Developed performance monitoring software providing real-time efficiency tracking and predictive maintenance alerts. Pricing model targets ROI within 18-24 months through fuel cost savings.

Technical Leadership

  • Patent applications filed for combustion chamber geometry and thermal recovery integration
  • Regulatory documentation prepared for emissions certification across target markets
  • Technical team scaling from founder-led to functional engineering organization
  • Manufacturing process documentation for contract manufacturing partnerships
